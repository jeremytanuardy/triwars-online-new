# Triwars Online - Game Overview

## Core Concept

**Genre:** 3-Faction MMORPG
**Inspiration:** RF Online (but modernized and balanced)
**Platform:** PC (Unity)
**Target Audience:** MMORPG players who love faction warfare

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

### Daily Activities (Solo/Small Group):
1. **Leveling** - Quests, grinding, dungeon runs
2. **Enhancement** - Upgrade gear with Card + Ore system
3. **Resource Gathering** - Mining, crafting materials
4. **Daily Dungeons** - Instanced PvE content

### Weekly Activities (Large Group):
1. **Faction War** (2x per week, scheduled)
   - Capture territory
   - Control resource points
   - Epic 50v50v50 battles
   - Rewards: Faction currency, rare materials

2. **Guild Wars** - Guild vs Guild battles
3. **World Bosses** - Server-wide events

---

## Progression Systems

### Character Progression:
- **Level Cap:** 100 (launch), expandable
- **Stat Distribution:** Classic STR/DEX/INT/VIT
- **Skill Points:** Unlock and upgrade abilities
- **Class Advancement:** None (pick once, commit)

### Equipment Progression:
- **Gear Tiers:** Common ‚Üí Uncommon ‚Üí Rare ‚Üí Epic ‚Üí Legendary
- **Enhancement:** +0 to +9 (safe), +10 to +20 (risky but no break)
- **Cards:** Socket cards into gear for bonus stats
- **Ore:** Elemental upgrades for weapons

### Faction Progression:
- **Faction Rank:** Individual contribution rank
- **Territory Bonuses:** Buffs based on controlled areas
- **Faction Shop:** Special items for faction currency

---

## Economy

### Currency Types:
1. **Gold** - Standard currency (PvE drops, quests)
2. **Faction Points** - Earned from faction wars
3. **Premium Currency** - Bought with real money (cosmetics only)

### Player Trading:
- **Marketplace** - Player-to-player auction house
- **Direct Trade** - Face-to-face trading
- **NO Trade Tax** for basic items

- **Guild Level:** Unlock perks
- **Guild Base:** Customizable headquarters
- **Guild Wars:** Weekly competitive content

- Global chat (faction-only)

## Monetization (NO P2W)

### What You CAN Buy:
- Cosmetic skins
- Mounts (same speed as free ones)
- Emotes & effects
- Character slots
- Bank expansion
- Name change

### What You CANNOT Buy:
- Stat advantages
- Gear
- Enhancement success boosters
- Experience boosters
- Anything that affects combat

---

## Key Design Pillars

1. **Fair Competition** - No P2W, skill-based combat
2. **Respect Player Time** - Scheduled wars, no 24/7 grind requirement
3. **Faction Identity** - Each race feels unique and meaningful
4. **Accessibility** - Easy to learn, hard to master
5. **Community First** - Guild/faction cooperation is rewarded
