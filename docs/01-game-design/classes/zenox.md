Zenox is the tech-focused race with 4 distinct classes.

---

## 1. Sentinel (Warrior Melee)

**Role:** Tank
**Weapon Type:** One-handed weapon + Shield
**Armor Type:** Heavy

### Overview
The Sentinel is Zenox's frontline defender, utilizing advanced shielding technology and melee combat protocols.

### Core Stats
- **High:** HP, Defense
- **Medium:** Strength
- **Low:** Speed, Magic

### Key Abilities
- **Shield Protocol:** Increase defense and aggro generation
- **Tech Barrier:** Energy shield that absorbs damage
- **Charge Protocol:** Rush forward and stun enemies
- **Emergency Repair:** Self-heal ability

### Playstyle
Tanky frontline fighter focused on protecting allies and controlling enemy aggro.

---

## 2. Energy Manipulator (Mage-Tech)

**Role:** Ranged Magic DPS
**Weapon Type:** Energy Staff
**Armor Type:** Light

### Overview
Masters of energy manipulation, combining technology with elemental forces.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Magic Attack, Energy
- **Medium:** Intelligence
- **Low:** HP, Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Energy Beam:** Channeled laser beam attack
- **Plasma Burst:** AoE explosion
- **Overcharge:** Increase magic damage temporarily
- **Energy Shield:** Defensive bubble

### Playstyle
Glass cannon mage with high burst damage and energy-based attacks.

---

## 3. Scout (Ranger DPS)

**Role:** Ranged Physical DPS
**Weapon Type:** Dual Pistols / Energy Rifle
**Armor Type:** Medium

### Overview
Fast-moving reconnaissance unit with high mobility and sustained ranged damage.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Speed, Dexterity
- **Medium:** Physical Attack
- **Low:** HP

### Key Abilities
- **Rapid Fire:** Fast attack speed buff
- **Tactical Retreat:** Dash backwards + speed boost
- **Precision Shot:** High damage single target
- **Stealth Mode:** Temporary invisibility

### Playstyle
High mobility kiting playstyle with sustained DPS.

---

## 4. Siege Engineer (Specialist - Launcher & Drone)

**Role:** Support/DPS Hybrid
**Weapon Type:** Launcher (Heavy weapon)
**Armor Type:** Medium

### Overview
Deploys drones and heavy weaponry for area control and support.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Utility, AoE Damage
- **Medium:** Physical Attack, Intelligence
- **Low:** Speed

### Key Abilities
- **Deploy Turret:** Stationary turret that attacks enemies
- **Missile Barrage:** Long-range AoE attack
- **Repair Drone:** Deploy drone that heals allies
- **EMP Blast:** Disable enemy tech temporarily

### Playstyle
Strategic support class that controls battlefield with deployables.

---

## Class Synergy

**Recommended Party Composition:**
- Sentinel (Tank)
- Energy Manipulator (Magic DPS)
- Scout (Physical DPS)
- Siege Engineer (Support)

**Faction Strength:** Tech/Ranged focus with good balance of roles.
