Sorcerion is the mystic magic-focused race with 4 powerful classes.

## 1. Berserker (Warrior Melee)

**Role:** Melee DPS/Tank Hybrid
**Weapon Type:** Two-handed Axe / Hammer
**Armor Type:** Heavy

### Overview
Rage-fueled warrior that sacrifices HP for devastating damage output.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Physical Attack, HP
- **Medium:** Strength, Defense
- **Low:** Speed

### Key Abilities
- **Rage Mode:** Sacrifice HP for increased damage
- **Bloodlust:** Attack speed increases as HP decreases
- **Crushing Blow:** High damage single target
- **Intimidating Roar:** AoE fear/debuff

### Playstyle
High-risk high-reward berserker with HP-sacrifice mechanics.

---

## 2. Shadow Ranger (Ranger DPS)

**Role:** Ranged Physical DPS + Stealth
**Weapon Type:** Dual Daggers + Throwing Knives
**Armor Type:** Light

### Overview
Assassin-style ranger that blends stealth with ranged attacks.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Dexterity, Critical Damage
- **Medium:** Physical Attack, Speed
- **Low:** HP, Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Shadow Step:** Teleport behind target + backstab
- **Poison Blade:** DoT damage over time
- **Vanish:** Enter stealth mode
- **Shadow Clone:** Create decoy

### Playstyle
Stealth-based assassin with burst damage from shadows.

---

## 3. Warlock (Mage)

**Role:** Ranged Magic DPS + DoT
**Weapon Type:** Staff + Orb
**Armor Type:** Light

### Overview
Dark magic practitioner with powerful curses and damage-over-time spells.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Magic Attack, Intelligence
- **Medium:** Energy
- **Low:** HP, Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Shadow Bolt:** Fast magic projectile
- **Curse of Agony:** Strong DoT spell
- **Life Drain:** Steal HP from enemy
- **Dark Ritual:** Sacrifice HP for energy/power

### Playstyle
DoT-focused mage with life manipulation abilities.

---

## 4. Inferno Summoner (Specialist - Summon Mythical Beast)

**Role:** Summoner/Pet Class
**Weapon Type:** Summoning Tome
**Armor Type:** Light

### Overview
Commands mythical beasts to fight alongside them. Can summon powerful creatures for various situations.

### Core Stats (Summoner)
- **High:** Intelligence, Summoning Power
- **Medium:** Magic Attack
- **Low:** HP, Physical Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Summon Familiar:** Basic permanent pet
- **Summon Elemental:** Temporary powerful elemental (Fire/Ice/Lightning)
- **Summon Beast Lord:** Ultimate summon (limited duration, high power)
- **Pet Command:** Direct control of summon abilities

### Summoning Types
- **Tank Pet:** High HP, holds aggro
- **DPS Pet:** High damage, glass cannon
- **Support Pet:** Buffs/heals allies

### Playstyle
Pet management class that fights through summoned creatures. **This is Sorcerion's signature class.**

---

## Class Synergy

**Recommended Party Composition:**
- Berserker (Melee DPS/Off-tank)
- Shadow Ranger (Stealth DPS)
- Warlock (Magic DPS/DoT)
- Inferno Summoner (Summoner/Support via pets)

**Faction Strength:** High magic damage with unique summoning and stealth mechanics.
