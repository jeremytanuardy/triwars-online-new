# ⚔️ Terran Classes

Terran is the balanced human race with 4 versatile classes.

---

## 1. Blade Specialist (Warrior Melee)

**Role:** Melee DPS
**Weapon Type:** Dual Swords / Two-handed Sword
**Armor Type:** Medium

### Overview
Master swordsman with high burst damage and critical hit potential.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Physical Attack, Critical Rate
- **Medium:** Speed, HP
- **Low:** Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Blade Dance:** Multi-hit combo attack
- **Critical Strike:** Guaranteed critical hit
- **Sword Mastery:** Passive attack speed increase
- **Whirlwind:** Spinning AoE attack

### Playstyle
High damage melee DPS with focus on critical hits and combos.

---

## 2. Marksman (Ranger DPS)

**Role:** Ranged Physical DPS
**Weapon Type:** Bow / Crossbow
**Armor Type:** Light

### Overview
Precision ranged attacker with long-range capabilities and trap skills.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Physical Attack, Range
- **Medium:** Dexterity, Critical Rate
- **Low:** HP, Defense

### Key Abilities
- **Headshot:** High damage + stun on critical
- **Multi-Shot:** Attack multiple targets
- **Trap Setup:** Deploy traps on ground
- **Eagle Eye:** Increase range and accuracy

### Playstyle
Long-range sniper with high single-target damage.

---

## 3. Technomancer (Mage-Tech Hybrid)

**Role:** Hybrid DPS/Support
**Weapon Type:** Tech Staff + Gun
**Armor Type:** Medium

### Overview
Unique hybrid class combining magic and technology for versatile combat.

### Core Stats
- **High:** Intelligence, Adaptability
- **Medium:** Magic Attack, Physical Attack
- **Low:** HP

### Key Abilities
- **Tech Blast:** Magic + Physical hybrid attack
- **Elemental Bullet:** Enchant weapon with element
- **Support Protocol:** Buff allies with tech
- **Overload:** High damage ultimate with cooldown

### Playstyle
Flexible hybrid that adapts to different situations.

---

## 4. MAU Pilot (Specialist - Mecha Units)

**Role:** Transforming Specialist
**Weapon Type:** Pilot Form: Pistol | MAU Form: Heavy Weapons
**Armor Type:** Variable

### Overview
Can summon and pilot MAU (Massive Assault Unit) for devastating power. RF Online's iconic mech warfare.

### Core Stats (Pilot Mode)
- **Medium:** All stats balanced
- Can transform into MAU

### Core Stats (MAU Mode)
- **High:** HP, Attack, Defense
- **Low:** Speed, Duration limited

### Key Abilities
- **Summon MAU:** Transform into mech (limited duration)
- **Rocket Barrage:** Heavy AoE damage
- **Siege Mode:** Stationary form with increased damage
- **Emergency Eject:** Return to pilot form with heal

### Playstyle
Unique transformation class with time-limited high power mode. **This is Terran's signature class.**

---

## Class Synergy

**Recommended Party Composition:**
- Blade Specialist (Melee DPS)
- Marksman (Ranged DPS)
- Technomancer (Hybrid Support)
- MAU Pilot (Heavy DPS/Tank when transformed)

**Faction Strength:** Balanced and versatile with iconic MAU warfare.
