## Faction Balance

All 3 factions are designed to be **equally viable** with different playstyles:

- **Zenox:** Tech/Ranged focus
- **Terran:** Balanced/Teamwork focus
- **Sorcerion:** Magic/Summon focus

No dominant faction meta - each has strengths and weaknesses.
